ABOUT KIKU


Kiku Bistro offers a fun and lively atmosphere  to enjoy creative, elegant sushi and other Asian  fare. Head Chef Patrick Liu and his wife are small business owners dedicated to the dynamic Uptown Minneapolis neighborhood where they make their home. Check out the video to the right, and then wander over to the 'Specials' page to meet Kiku Bear, Pat's BFF who pops in every couple of  months to get his sushi fix. Also, you can follow  us on Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube by clicking  on the icons below. We hope to meet you soon!
